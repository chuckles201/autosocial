# Myanmar Coup - 2021 Key Developments:

1. On February 1, 2021, Myanmar's military (Tatmadaw) deposed democratically elected NLD members, transferring power to Senior General Min Aung Hlaing just before the new parliament was to be sworn in.

2. The military declared the November 2020 election results invalid and announced a year-long state of emergency, with promises of new elections that never materialized.

3. President Win Myint and State Counsellor Aung San Suu Kyi were detained on what analysts describe as dubious charges, with Suu Kyi facing multiple additional charges
